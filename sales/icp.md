# Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

The precise definition of who is worth pursuing. A sharp ICP is the highest-leverage document in
sales: it makes outreach relevant, qualification fast, and time well spent.

> **Status: starter draft.** The structure is complete; the specifics below are reasonable
> starting assumptions for Eik & Friends and should be refined by Jonathan with real win/loss
> data. Update as patterns emerge.

## In one sentence
Businesses in Norway (initially the Oslo region) that host or sponsor in-person gatherings for
their teams, clients, or partners, and that value a memorable, well-run experience over the
cheapest option.

## Firmographics
| Attribute | Ideal | Acceptable | Avoid |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Company size | 50–500 employees | 20–1,000 | Solo / very large bureaucracies |
| Location | Oslo & greater Oslo | Norway | Outside delivery range |
| Industry | Tech, professional services, finance, creative | Most B2B | Sectors with no event budget |
| Event frequency | Recurring (quarterly+) | Annual | One-off only |

## The buyer (who we talk to)
- **Primary:** People/HR leads, Office/Culture managers, Executive Assistants, Marketing managers.
- **Economic buyer:** Department head or founder/exec for larger budgets.
- **Why they buy:** team morale, client entertainment, brand moments, celebrating milestones.

## Triggers (good reasons to reach out *now*)
- New office, expansion, or funding round.
- A hiring surge or onboarding wave.
- Seasonal moments: summer party, Christmas (julebord), kickoff, anniversaries.
- A product launch or client appreciation event.
- A previous venue/partner let them down.

## Value we provide
- Memorable, well-produced experiences without the client doing the legwork.
- Reliability and taste — it reflects well on the host.
- Local relationships and venues that are hard to access alone.

## Disqualifiers (politely pass)
- No budget or seeking the cheapest possible option above all.
- Outside our delivery range or timeline.
- One-off with no relationship potential and poor margin.
- Misaligned values / unreasonable demands.

## Partnership ICP (for collaborations)
For restaurant/venue/brand partners (see
[`playbook-restaurant-partnerships.md`](playbook-restaurant-partnerships.md)):
- Serves a similar premium, corporate clientele.
- Complementary (not directly competing) offering.
- Reliable, quality-focused, good reputation.
- Open to reciprocal referrals or co-marketing.

## How to use this
- **Outreach:** only target accounts matching the firmographics + a real trigger.
- **Qualification:** score inbound against this; disqualify fast and kindly.
- **Refinement:** when we win or lose, note *why* and feed it back here.
